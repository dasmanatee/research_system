Data center construction rolls into 2026

2025 may have come to an end, but the data center construction boom continued.

Published Jan. 5, 2026
By Construction Dive Staff
  • Share
    • Copy link
    • Email
    • LinkedIn
    • X/Twitter

  • Add us on Google

The biggest single construction news topic of 2025? The boom in data center construction.

Even as fears of a bubble persisted in the sector to end the year, new data center groundbreakings, plans for additional developments and legislation to streamline permitting kept coming as well. Read on for the latest data center construction news to kick off 2026.

  • Data center developer breaks ground on $3B Virginia campus

    By Sebastian Obando Dec. 1, 2025

    The 900-megawatt campus will span multiple phases with full buildout expected by 2035, according to CleanArc Data Centers. Read the full article

  • Construction planning falls again, but data center boom continues

    By Sebastian Obando Dec. 5, 2025

    Megaprojects are boosting the building pipeline as overall momentum dipped for a second consecutive month, according to Dodge Construction Network. Read the full article

  • As EPA moves to fast-track data centers, some cities are moving to slow them down

    By Robyn Griggs Lawrence Dec. 18, 2025

    The EPA is streamlining Clean Air Act permitting to accelerate development. Will local communities pay the price? Read the full article

  • $15B Stargate data center breaks ground in Wisconsin

    By Sebastian Obando Dec. 19, 2025

    Crews officially began the first phase of construction on the Oracle, OpenAI and Vantage Data Centers facility and expect to complete the project, dubbed Lighthouse, in 2028. Read the full article

  • House passes bill that could fast-track AI infrastructure projects

    By Makenzie Holland Dec. 22, 2025

    The SPEED Act aims to cut red tape in order to meet soaring energy demand and growing competition in the global AI race, according to the bill’s sponsors. Read the full article

  • Jacobs starts $10B Louisiana data center

    By Sebastian Obando Dec. 22, 2025

    Energy infrastructure provider Hut 8 tapped the Dallas-based contractor for engineering, procurement and construction management. Read the full article
